Get Involved

Power statewide. Built by people who show up.

Ohio Pride needs volunteers and interns in all 88 counties. Tell us how you want to help, and we will plug you in.

Endorsements move the needle. Voters decide elections. Volunteers do both jobs. Whether you have an hour a month or ten hours a week, there is a place for you on this team.

We are building a statewide network of organizers, captains, and skill-based volunteers who can power candidate work, voter mobilization, and our legislative scorecard. The roles below are the most common ways people plug in. Pick whatever fits.

Field and Canvassing
Knock doors, drop literature, and turn out voters for endorsed candidates.
Phone and Text Banking
Reach voters from your couch. Scripts and lists provided.
Pride Tabling
Sign up volunteers and register voters at Pride events across Ohio in June.
Social Amplification
Share, repost, and help our content reach voters who need to see it.
Skills-Based
Writers, designers, photographers, attorneys, data folks, and more. Use what you do best.
House Party Host
Open your home for a small fundraiser or organizing meeting in your community.
Local Captain
Lead volunteer coordination in your city or county. Higher commitment, higher impact.
Day-of Logistics
Show up for events, rallies, and press conferences when we need hands on deck.
Internships and Fellowships

Summer and Fall 2026 team positions

Five team positions at Ohio's first statewide LGBTQ+ political action committee. Summer 2026 placements are filling now with immediate start dates available. All positions are unpaid academic-credit placements; the PAC signs your university's learning agreement and the supervisor of record completes midterm and final evaluations. Mileage is reimbursed at the IRS standard rate for required in-person travel.

Academic credit Summer 2026 rolling Fall 2026 priority July 15 8 to 15 hrs/week

Department chairs and faculty internship coordinators can email zach@ohiopride.org to discuss learning agreements, evaluation protocols, or approved-site-list inclusion.
