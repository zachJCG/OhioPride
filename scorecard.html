Scoring Methodology

Every legislator gets a weighted 0–100 score on LGBTQ+ equality, built from four inputs and resolved directly from the Supabase roll-call database. The grade scale runs A+ through F.

  • 40% Floor Votes — chamber passage, concurrence, and veto overrides.
  • 10% Committee Votes — reports that advance or hold a bill.
  • 30% Sponsorship — primary (±3) and co-sponsorship (±2) of tracked bills.
  • 20% News & Statements — public statements and floor speeches.

Event-stage multipliers (veto override 1.25×, passage 1.00×, committee 0.75×, amendment 0.50×, introduction 0.25×) apply inside each input. Full details — the SQL pipeline, event weights, verification statuses, intersectional framing, and grade scale — live on the methodology page.
