Scoring Methodology

Score Weights

  • Floor Votes (50%): +1 per pro-equality vote, -1 per anti-equality vote
  • Sponsorship (30%): +3 primary sponsor pro bill, +2 co-sponsor pro bill; -3 primary sponsor anti bill, -2 co-sponsor anti bill
  • News/Statements (20%): +1 to +3 for pro-LGBTQ+ advocacy; -1 to -3 for anti-LGBTQ+ rhetoric

Raw scores are normalized to a 0-100 scale using the formula: max(0, min(100, 50 + raw * 5))
