What This Bill Does

Bans diversity, equity, and inclusion offices, programs, and required DEI training at Ohio's public universities and community colleges. Restricts faculty collective bargaining and mandates classroom 'intellectual diversity' reviews. Signed by Gov. DeWine on March 28, 2025.

Why It Matters for LGBTQ+ Ohioans

SB 1, the Advance Ohio Higher Education Act, bans diversity, equity, and inclusion offices, programs, and required DEI training at Ohio's public universities and community colleges. It restricts faculty collective bargaining and mandates classroom 'intellectual diversity' reviews. The bill passed the Senate 21-11 on February 12, 2025 (with Sens. Blessing III and Patton crossing), cleared the House 59-34 on March 19, 2025, was concurred 20-11 on March 26, 2025, and was signed into law on March 28, 2025.
