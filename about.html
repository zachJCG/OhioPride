About Ohio Pride PAC

Building political power for LGBTQ+ Ohioans at every level of government.

Our Mission

Ohio Pride PAC is Ohio's Political Action Committee for LGBTQ+ Equality. We mobilize voters, endorse pro-equality candidates, and advocate for the rights of LGBTQ+ Ohioans at every level of government.

We exist to build durable political power for the LGBTQ+ community in Ohio — electing leaders who will defend our rights, tracking and fighting anti-LGBTQ+ legislation in the Statehouse, and educating voters about what's at stake in every election.
