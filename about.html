Who We Are

Building Political Power for LGBTQ+ Ohio

Ohio Pride is a political action committee dedicated to building political power for LGBTQ+ equality across every corner of Ohio. We endorse pro-equality candidates regardless of party, mobilize voters, and fight anti-LGBTQ+ legislation in the Ohio Statehouse.

Founded in 2026, Ohio Pride is a non-partisan political committee focused singularly on electing candidates who will protect and advance the rights of LGBTQ+ Ohioans and their families. We are funded entirely by grassroots supporters and community allies.

Why We're Different

The Electoral Work Others Can't Do

Ohio has strong LGBTQ+ advocacy groups, and national PACs that back LGBTQ+ candidates. Ohio Pride is different: a nonpartisan, state-level political action committee that endorses any pro-equality candidate — LGBTQ+ or ally — who shares our values. As a PAC, we complement that advocacy work with tools the nonprofit structure isn't built for:

  • Endorse pro-equality candidates of any party — LGBTQ+ or ally — at every level of Ohio government, from school board to statewide races.
  • Campaign in direct support of pro-equality issues and ballot measures, not just candidates.
  • Score and grade legislators on their LGBTQ+ voting records, publicly.
  • Run independent expenditure campaigns to support pro-equality candidates and oppose anti-equality incumbents.
  • Take direct political action during election cycles, not just lobby between them.
Advocacy organizations educate and lobby. Ohio Pride builds on that work by putting money and power behind the candidates and causes that will act.

Why We're Needed Now

A Critical Moment for LGBTQ+ Ohio

The 136th Ohio General Assembly has introduced a wave of anti-LGBTQ+ legislation, from forced outing bills to restrictions on healthcare and drag performance. At the same time, 2026 is a historic election year with all five statewide executive offices on the ballot, a special U.S. Senate election, and critical statehouse races.
