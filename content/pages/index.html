What We Do

🎯

Endorse Candidates

We identify, endorse, and contribute to pro-equality candidates at every level, from statehouse races to statewide offices. 2026 is a historic election year.

📚

Educate Voters

We score every Ohio legislator on their LGBTQ+ equality record and publish plain-language guides, so voters know who's protecting equality and who's voting against it.

View the Scorecard →
⚖️

Advocate on LGBTQ+ Issues

We track and fight anti-LGBTQ+ legislation in the Ohio Statehouse, from forced outing bills to drag bans, and push for pro-equality policy.
