What This Bill Does

SB 113 is the Senate companion to HB 155. It requires school boards to adopt and enforce a policy prohibiting multiple DEI-related structures and practices, including DEI training courses, DEI offices/departments, and certain contracting.

Impact on LGBTQIA+ Ohioans

Like HB 155, this type of structure can suppress LGBTQIA+-inclusive initiatives even when framed as general "DEI," because LGBTQIA+ inclusion work is frequently operationalized through trainings, support offices, and policy review. Having companion bills in both chambers increases the chance of legislative action.

Legal & Constitutional Risks

  • Undefined Key Terms — Same vagueness concerns as HB 155
  • Chilling Effects — Potential to induce viewpoint-based enforcement in school governance
  • Impacts hinge on how schools and enforcement bodies interpret "DEI" broadly
