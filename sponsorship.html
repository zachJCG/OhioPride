Inaugural Gala

Sponsorship Opportunities

Demonstrate your commitment to equality while gaining meaningful visibility to 400+ community leaders, elected officials, and allies from across Ohio.

Ohio Pride PAC invites your organization to sponsor the Inaugural Gala — a black-tie evening celebrating LGBTQ+ identity, community, and political power in Ohio. The event features a red carpet arrival, cocktail reception, seated gala dinner, keynote program with political leaders, high-quality drag entertainment, and a DJ-driven afterparty.

Corporate sponsorships are permissible contributions to Ohio Pride PAC under Ohio campaign finance law. Sponsorship demonstrates your organization's values while providing direct access to an engaged audience of business professionals, community leaders, and elected officials.
