# Endorsement Application FAQ

For candidates considering an Ohio Pride PAC endorsement.

---

## What is Ohio Pride PAC?

Ohio Pride PAC is a state-level political action committee registered with the Ohio Secretary of State. We endorse pro-equality candidates, mobilize voters, and fight bills that target LGBTQ+ Ohioans. We are independent and partisan-agnostic. We endorse based on commitments and record, not on party.

## What does an Ohio Pride PAC endorsement get me?

A few things, depending on your race:

- **Visibility.** Your name and campaign appear on our public endorsements page at `ohiopride.org/endorsements`, where voters look to see who shares their values.
- **Voter mobilization.** We work to move LGBTQ+ Ohioans and allies in your district. The exact form depends on your race and timing.
- **Coalition.** You become part of a network of pro-equality candidates we coordinate across the state. Endorsed candidates are invited to our regular coordination calls.
- **Direct line.** You get a direct line to the Director for any rapid response moments your campaign faces.

## Who reviews applications?

Our Screening Committee reads every application closely. The Board of Ohio Pride PAC has final approval on all endorsements. Both groups are bound by confidentiality, so unpublished applications stay private.

## How long does the process take?

Plan for two to four weeks from submission to decision. We may follow up by email with clarifying questions or to schedule a brief candidate conversation. Endorsements are voted on a rolling basis, not in one big batch, so timing depends on when your application reaches the Board calendar.

## What are you looking for?

Strong, consistent, public support for LGBTQ+ equality. The application asks five yes-or-no questions on the issues that matter most to us:

1. Statewide nondiscrimination protections (the Ohio Fairness Act)
2. Opposition to legislation restricting gender-affirming care or expression
3. A statewide ban on conversion therapy
4. Inclusive K-12 schools, including opposition to forced outing policies
5. Voting against rollbacks and broad religious exemptions used to discriminate

We also want to see how your equality priorities connect to the broader case you are making to voters: healthcare, education, economic opportunity, rural community needs. The strongest applications show that LGBTQ+ equality is part of a larger vision for Ohio, not a checkbox.

## Will my responses be public?

Yes. By submitting, you certify that your responses can be shared publicly with voters. If you are endorsed, your bio and campaign link will be featured on `ohiopride.org/endorsements`. If you are not endorsed, your application stays private with the Screening Committee and Board.

## Can I update my application after submitting?

Yes, just email `screening@ohiopride.org` with the change. We accept revisions until the Board vote. After endorsement, send updates to your bio, campaign link, or contact info to the same address and we will refresh your card on the public page.

## What if you decline to endorse me?

We will tell you. We try to give you a brief reason so you understand the decision. Declining today does not mean declining forever. Candidates who shift their record or commitments can reapply in a future cycle.

## Do I need to be out as LGBTQ+ to apply?

No. We endorse pro-equality candidates regardless of identity. The application does ask whether you are out as LGBTQ+ for our internal context. That answer is not displayed publicly unless you give us specific permission to highlight it.

## How long does the application take?

About 15 to 20 minutes if you have your campaign details ready. The form autosaves locally as you go, so you can step away and return on the same device. We recommend drafting your longer responses in a separate document first and pasting them in.

## How do I contact you?

Email `screening@ohiopride.org` for anything related to your application or endorsement. For general Ohio Pride PAC questions, contact the Director at `zach@ohiopride.org`.

## Apply

The application lives at `ohiopride.org/endorsement/screening`. We review on a rolling basis. The sooner you apply, the more time we have to coordinate on rollout if endorsed.
